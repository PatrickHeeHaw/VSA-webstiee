About OU Vietnamese Student Association

Connecting Culture, Community, and Campus

Our Mission

The Vietnamese Student Association at the University of Oklahoma is dedicated to promoting Vietnamese culture, fostering community among Vietnamese and Vietnamese-American students, and creating cross-cultural understanding across campus.

Through social events, cultural celebrations, and educational initiatives, we aim to create a supportive environment where students can connect with their heritage while sharing it with the broader university community.

Our History

Founded in 2005, the OU Vietnamese Student Association began as a small group of students seeking to maintain their cultural connections while away from home. Over the years, we have grown into one of the most active cultural organizations on campus.

From our humble beginnings with just 15 members, we now proudly represent over 100 students. Our annual Lunar New Year celebration has become a campus-wide event, and our community service initiatives have made lasting impacts both locally and internationally.
